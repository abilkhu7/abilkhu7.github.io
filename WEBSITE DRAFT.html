Dr. Amrit Bilkhu

Your family optometrist striving to protect vision and eye health for all ages.
scroll

Dr. Bilkhu offers a range of eye services that fit all of your visual needs.

Adult eye exams

Experience a thorough comprehensive eye exam on a regular basis to maintain good eye health and identify risk factors for ocular diseases.
OHIP eye exams

OHIP insurance provides annual comprehensive eye exams for children 19 and under, seniors 65 and older, and adults with certain medical or eye conditions.
Glasses and contact lenses

Get a custom prescription fit just for you! Discuss options for digital lenses, progressive lenses, daily disposable contacts, multifocal contacts, and more.
Ocular health exams

This includes eye emergencies and dilated eye exams to thoroughly assess for glaucoma, retinal diseases, and medical conditions such as high blood pressure and high cholesterol.
Binocular vision & vision therapy

Thorough evaluations and individualized therapy programs for lazy eye, focusing problems, poor eye-hand coordination, concussion symptoms, or vision-related reading challenges.
Dry eye management

Take control of your dry eye symptoms by learning about high quality dry eye products that have superior performance to over-the-counter options.
Dr. Bilkhu is a Brampton native and takes pride in serving her community, providing optometric services to families across the GTA.

Dr. Bilkhu strives to make every single patient feel comfortable in the exam room and loves educating her patients on the importance of an eye exam. She ensures that her patients have thorough understanding of their own medical or eye conditions by showing photographs of their own eyes.

Dr. Bilkhu has a passion for identifying and managing vision-related learning disorders in school-aged children. She is an advocate for these patients, and works hard to provide specialized glasses prescriptions, school letters for accommodations, and vision rehabilitation programs to help achieve their best academic performance.

After clinical hours, Dr. Bilkhu writes optometric articles for Eyes on Eyecare. She is also the creater of the popular optometry podcast, Four Eyes. She interviews influential optometrists and ophthalmologists who are making a difference in the profession, and shares her optometric clinical knowledge along with her co-hosts.

2015

BSc Health Sciences - Wilfrid Laurier University

Dr. Bilkhu specifically chose this program for the purpose of improving human health and well-being.

2019

Doctor of Optometry - Illinois College of Optometry

Dr. Bilkhu gained clinical experience in all areas of optometry throughout the program. She also completed externships focusing on ocular disease management in Chicago, Los Angeles, and Daytona.

2020

Vision Therapy and Rehabilitation Residency - University of California, Berkeley

Throughout her residency education, Dr. Bilkhu managed patients with amblyopia, strabismus, binocular vision dysfunction, concussion, and vision-related learning challenges.
